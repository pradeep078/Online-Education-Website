Matter is found in 3 major states; solid, liquid and gas.   So what is matter made of ?. All matter is made of atoms.
  Atoms are the smallest particle of matter.
  They are so small that you cannot see them with your eyes or even with a standard microscope.
  A standard sheet of paper is about a million atoms thick. Science has come up with a technology to identify atoms called a scanning tunneling   microscope (STM)
which uses electricity to map atoms. There is more about atoms later, but first let's learn about the three states of matter.
